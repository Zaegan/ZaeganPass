ZaeganPass

Privacy Policy · Last updated: May 2026

Overview

ZaeganPass is a local password generator for Android. It is designed to collect no data about you. Everything happens on your device; nothing leaves it.

Data we collect

None. ZaeganPass does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, usage data, analytics, or telemetry. There are no servers, no accounts, and no network connections made by this application.

Generated passwords

Generated passwords are never written to disk, never logged, and never transmitted. They live only in memory while displayed on screen, and on the Android system clipboard if you copy one. The app does not keep a history of passwords you have generated.

What is stored on your device

ZaeganPass stores only your generator preferences — desired password length, which character classes to include (uppercase, lowercase, digits, symbols), and your theme choice. These settings are kept in private SharedPreferences on your device. No passwords, accounts, or other personal data are stored.

Permissions

ZaeganPass requests no permissions. The app does not access the internet, your contacts, your files, your camera, your location, or any other device resource beyond its own private storage and the system clipboard (for the copy-password action you trigger).

Third-party services

ZaeganPass does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or crash reporting tools. However, as with all apps distributed through Google Play, Google may collect certain device and crash information through Google Play Services in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This data collection is outside of ZaeganPass's control and is not initiated by the app itself.

Users who wish to avoid this entirely can sideload ZaeganPass by downloading the latest release APK directly from the GitHub repository instead of installing through Google Play.

Open source

ZaeganPass is fully open source. You can inspect the complete source code at github.com/Zaegan/ZaeganPass.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the GitHub repository linked above.
